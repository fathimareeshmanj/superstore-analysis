# superstore-analysis

**Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)**

**Project Description**

Developed an interactive Sales and Profit Analysis Dashboard using Power BI to analyze business performance across categories, segments, regions, shipping modes, and order priorities. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales trends, profitability, customer distribution, and operational efficiency, enabling data-driven decision-making.

 **Project Objectives**
 

Analyze overall sales and profit performance

Compare sales vs profit across product categories

Understand customer behavior by segment and ship mode

Identify high-performing and low-performing sub-categories

Enable dynamic filtering by country, region, city, market, category, and segment



 **Key Insights from Dashboard**
 

Total Sales: 12.64M

Total Profit: 1.47M

Technology category generates the highest sales and profit

Consumer segment contributes the largest share of customers and quantity

Standard Class is the most preferred shipping mode (~60%)

Medium priority orders dominate total order quantity

Sub-categories like Binders, Storage, and Art show high order counts


 **Dashboard Features**
 

KPI Cards for Total Sales & Total Profit

Sales & Profit by Category (Bar + Line Combo Chart)

Customer Count by Segment (Waterfall Chart)

Customers by Ship Mode (Pie Chart)

Order Quantity by Priority (Donut Chart)

Sub-category Performance (Horizontal Bar Chart)

Product-level details using tables

Interactive slicers for deep analysis

Drill-through & tooltip-enabled visuals


 **Tools & Technologies Used**
 

Power BI Desktop

Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)

DAX (Measures & Calculations)

Data Modeling & Relationships

Interactive Visualizations


**Conclusion**



This Power BI dashboard provides a quick and interactive overview of sales, profit, customer segments, and order trends. It helps identify top-performing categories, preferred shipping modes, and high-demand segments, enabling clear insights for better business and operational decisions.
